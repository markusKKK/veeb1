Touring Diary: A sketchbook in your basket

19 APRIL 2023

"Touring Diary: A Sketchbook in Your Basket" offers a unique perspective on the joys and challenges of bicycle touring through the lens of art and creativity. This captivating series follows the adventures of a group of cyclists as they embark on epic journeys across diverse landscapes, capturing their experiences through sketches, drawings, and watercolors.

Each episode of "Touring Diary" documents a different leg of the cyclists' journey, from rugged mountain passes to serene coastal roads and bustling city streets. Viewers are invited to join the riders as they pedal through breathtaking scenery, encountering fascinating people and cultures along the way. What sets "Touring Diary" apart is its emphasis on artistic expression and storytelling. As the cyclists navigate winding roads and picturesque trails, they take breaks to sketch their surroundings, capturing the beauty of nature, the charm of small towns, and the excitement of unexpected encounters. These sketches serve as windows into the cyclists' experiences, offering glimpses of their thoughts, emotions, and observations throughout the journey. Through interviews and behind-the-scenes footage, viewers gain insight into the creative process behind the sketches, as well as the technical aspects of bicycle touring, such as gear selection, route planning, and camping tips. Expert artists and seasoned cyclists share their insights and advice, inspiring viewers to embrace their own creativity and embark on their own cycling adventures. "Touring Diary: A Sketchbook in Your Basket" celebrates the spirit of exploration, self-discovery, and camaraderie that defines bicycle touring. Whether you're an experienced cyclist looking for inspiration or a novice eager to learn more about the world of bike travel, this series offers a fresh perspective on the joys of slow travel and the power of art to enrich our lives.
